MiniSheet

Version v0.2.10

MiniSheet is a formula-driven mini spreadsheet for quick calculations.

Type a math problem, like 42+25, into the formula line and tap Result for an answer. Or create a formula like A+B/C and enter numbers in the grid to get multiple results for different sets of values.

For the best experience, click the share button and add to your homescreen.
